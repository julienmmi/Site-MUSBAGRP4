Permanent Collections

The permanent collection of European painting and sculpture is displayed in the Lacour and Bonheur wings, following a journey through the major periods of art history, the great centers and main movements from the 15th to the 20th century with works by Perugino, Titian, Veronese, Brueghel the Elder, Van Dyck, Rubens, Chardin, Delacroix, Corot, Rodin, Kokoschka, Picasso, Matisse...

Museum History

The Museum of Fine Arts of Bordeaux (MusBA) - labeled Museum of France - offers visitors a broad panorama of European art from the 15th to the 20th century. With more than 8,400 works (paintings, sculptures and graphic arts), it is one of the major fine arts collections in Nouvelle-Aquitaine.

Shop

The museum shop offers a wide range of objects, postcards, books, magazines, jewelry, scarves, games and various products related to the museum's collections, temporary exhibitions and more broadly the themes of Fine Arts and art history.
